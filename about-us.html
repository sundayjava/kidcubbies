Kid Cubbies is a platform built with families and childcare providers in mind. We understand that choosing the right care for your child is a big decision, and we’re here to make that process simpler, safer, and more personal.

Whether you're a parent looking for a cozy home daycare or a provider hoping to reach more families, Kid Cubbies is your space to connect, explore, and grow together.

Our Mission

To make high-quality, trusted home childcare accessible to every family by empowering providers and supporting parents through technology, connection, and care.

Our Vision

A future where every child has access to a nurturing space to grow and every parent has peace of mind in their home childcare choices.

What Makes Us Different

Family-Centered Approach

We prioritize what matters most to parents — trust, simplicity, and peace of mind — making the search for care easy and stress-free.

Supportive to Providers

Kid Cubbies empowers daycare owners with the tools and visibility they need to grow, connect, and thrive in their care journey.

Verified & Safe

All listed providers go through a careful vetting process, giving families confidence in the safety and quality of every match.

Flexible Options

From home-based care to structured programs, families can explore a range of daycare types that suit their unique needs and schedules.

Community-Driven

We foster real relationships between families and caregivers, creating a supportive, trusted community for everyone involved.

Simple, Modern Platform

Our tech makes finding and managing care seamless — from smart filters to direct messaging and easy onboarding.
